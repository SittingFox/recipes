Ingredients:
 - peanut butter
 - jelly (grape or strawberry)
 - 2 slices of bread

Directions:
 1. Place bread onto a plate.
 
 2. Using a knife, spread your desired amount of peanut butter on one slice of 
    bread.
 
 3. On top of the other slice, spread as much jelly as you'd like.
 
 4. Lift up the slice with peanut butter on it and set it on top of the other, 
    such that the peanut butter and jelly sides face each other.
